Admission

The entrance is free for all

There are guided tours of the museum that leave every hour. These tours are 7$ per person and include a handy printed guide of the museum.

If you would like to organise a guided tour for your group of 6 or more people, please contact us to arrange the tour.

Accessibility

Our museum strives to be accessible to everyone. There are wheelchair accessibility ramps. It also has audio guides and braille display signs for the visually impaired.
